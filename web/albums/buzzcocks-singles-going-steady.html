Buzzcocks, Singles Going Steady

I.R.S., 1979, punk

B, 1966: A great album of fast, well constructed songs. A great raw energy, mixed with a pop sensibility. Track pick is the ever hooky ‘Ever Fallen In Love (With Someone One You Shouldn't've)’.

C, 2002: Fun album; great energy. Track picks are “promises” and “autonomy”.

H, 1951: Really great stuff. Energetic, witty. No dated political references (at least that I noticed). Cynical, but not nihilistic. I'm going to go with “Whatever Happened To…?” for my track pick.
